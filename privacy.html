Privacy

Read ecommerce osCommerce privacy

As the user on this website you can create the account and use your profile from your social network accounts. Once you create your account you will be able to shop faster, be up to date on an orders status and keep track of the orders you have previously made.

You can find an item on the website using search tool, sort the items by date, name, weight, price, model, manufacturer, brand and category. You can also set up how many items you want to show.

You can choose different currencies and languages on the website.

Purchasing process is simple and straight forward. While purchasing an item you can add it to “My wish list”, add an item to cart, proceed to checkout, fill in the required details there and choose different payment methods (PayPal (including Credit and Debit Cards), Credit Card or Bank Card (Processed by Sage Pay), Cash on Delivery or LiqPay).

After placing the order you will be notified about it via email. Each time the status of your order is changed you will be notified about it via email as well. Finally we take your security on this website very seriously.

All your personal information will be securely stored and under no circumstances will be shared with anyone.
